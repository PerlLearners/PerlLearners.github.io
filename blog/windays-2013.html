Windays - UX and Design

Posted on 2013-05-06 in Design

From 22nd to 25th of April, Umag became the hometown of the biggest technology conference in Croatia - Windays13. More than a thousand people interested in Microsoft’s technologies attended, from system administrators and software developers to project managers. I had a privilege to hold a lecture about user experience and design.

Originally, the presentation was in Croatian with two hands-on demos. I translated it to English and added a few new slides from the demos to give it a bit more context. That way it’s much more accessible when viewed online.
