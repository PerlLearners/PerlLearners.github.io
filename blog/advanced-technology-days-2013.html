Advanced Technology Days 2013

Posted on 2013-12-04 in Design

The first day of Microsoft’s Advanced Technology Days is over. I gave a presentation about the design and development process of BliVakker mobile web shop. It was the same talk I gave on Webcamp more than a month ago, so you can find the slides in an earlier post.

Time slots were longer here so I had an opportunity to expand more on the server-side part of the story. The back-end was built using Microsoft’s technologies by Ignition Development from New Zealand. I briefly explained challenges and solutions they had encountered building such a complex web project, but I recommend you take a look at the technical report they prepared for this occasion.
