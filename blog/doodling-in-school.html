Doodling in school

Posted on 2016-04-13 in Design

Yesterday I tweeted that I doodled something after a long time. It struck me this morning that I became proficient in drawing while I was in school. There weren’t any drawing classes there, but the sheer amount of boring lectures was too much for a curious mind. My escape from that torture was filling any white space with doodles, comics, and sometimes, full page drawings.
