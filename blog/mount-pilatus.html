Mount Pilatus

Posted on 2015-08-23 in Travel

I climbed Mount Pilatus near Luzern, Switzerland yesterday. It was a long hike to around 2,100 meters above sea level. Views and nature were stunning and cowbells rang to complement the experience. The steepest cog railway in the world provided rest to exhausted, but happy, legs on the way down.
